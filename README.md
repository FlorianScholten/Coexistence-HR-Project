# Coexistence-HR-Project
With this tool we will improve the recruitment process. 

## Summary

With this tool we are going to revolutionize the recruitment process. We are going to find the right people for the righ vacancy and we are going to find the right vacancy for the right people. Based on personal preferences and career goals we do not only find the next job but a job which will bring you one step closer to your end goal of your career. In addition, we help companies to find the right and loyal talents - loyalty comes from both sides - delivering your work & providing the right environment to grow. 


## Background

Which problems does your idea solve? 
Companies struggle to find and keep the right people in their organization. 
Companies mark young talents as not loyal - but this is not the issue.
People do not have a clear plan with goals in mind where they would like to be - we support them. 
People do not find the right job & if they find the right job they are not satisfied over a longer period. 

How common or frequent is this problem? 
The problem is very common, especially for people between 20-45. 

What is your personal motivation? 
I search the right people for my teams. 
I see people around me struggling with their current jobs. 
I see my company and other companies struggling to find the right people, while getting thousands of applications per vacancy. 

Why is this topic important or interesting?
In the long term macroeconomically we need the right people in the right positions to further develop as humanity. 
We need to find solutions for current and future problems (climate change) we need to make sure people like what they do because they are good in it.
